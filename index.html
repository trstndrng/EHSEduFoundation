WHO ARE WE?

Our board of directors is made up of a diverse mix of students, staff, parents and community members. We consist of a banker, a lawyer, three upperclass students, school staff, local business owners and a district employee. All board members have direct ties to the community or EHS by being parents of students or alumni of Erie.
